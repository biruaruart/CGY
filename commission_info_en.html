Cancellation
When will a commission be cancelled?
If there is no response within 7 days after the first confirmation email is sent, the commission will be considered cancelled.
If the requested content is beyond the artist's current abilities, the commission will be declined and the client will be notified.

Commission Steps
01
Send your commission request
Once reviewed, the artist will reply via DM or email to confirm receipt and discuss the details and final pricing.
02
Payment
Full payment must be completed upfront. Work will only begin after full payment is received.
First paid, first served!
03
Sketch preview
A rough sketch will be sent via DM or email for your review.
You may request up to 2 free revisions at this stage. No major changes will be accepted after this point — please confirm everything you want during the sketch phase.
※ Surprise Box commissions cannot be revised unless there is an artist error (e.g. wrong eye color, wrong outfit details).
04
Final preview
A finished preview will be sent for your final review.
One last minor revision is allowed at this stage — limited to small adjustments only (e.g. coloring errors).
05
Commission complete!
The final files will be delivered to you via email.
You will receive: full-resolution PNG and watermarked PNG.
⚠
Important
Once the commission is completed and you have confirmed receipt (in any form), the transaction is considered final. No further revisions will be accepted, and the artwork may not be modified by the commissioner.
⚠
Regarding colors
The artist uses a rich, expressive color palette and generally does not replicate a character's original colors exactly. Please review the provided examples carefully before commissioning.
